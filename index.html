What is Product Management?

Product management is an organizational function that involves planning, forecasting, and marketing products at all stages of the product lifecycle. It involves a combination of market research, product development, and marketing to ensure that a product meets customer needs and is successful in the marketplace.

Why is Product Management Important?

Product management is important because it helps organizations develop products that meet customer needs and are successful in the marketplace. By conducting market research and developing a product roadmap, product managers can ensure that their products are aligned with customer needs and are profitable for the organization.
